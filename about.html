About me

About… about me hey? Well writing an about section on your self is kind of tricky but here it is anyway!

My name is Creepysin or Liam which is well, my real name. I was born on the 3rd of March 2004 in Australia, Queensland. I have never left the country and I have lived in the same city(town?) for my entire life (so far), but yes, I have gone to other cities before in case you’re wondering.

Sometime in 2009~2010 I was first introduced to a little game called Minecraft, dad was playing it on his computer, and I guess it looked interesting to me. Eventually I got my own computer that also had Minecraft on it, it was fun back then and kind of still is today. This would be the game that got me into gamming. Also, that computer... I kind of... well blew it up. I blew it up by flipping that big red switch on back of the computer on the power supply that old computers had. Stupid I know, but hey I was four!

I first started to code back in 2015 when I was in to Source SDK modding, even though all my old Source mods are complete shit. The only Source mod that I am happy with was a mod called Revenge and even still to this day it is not 100% complete (I scraped a lot of it in early 2018 due to the old map being… bad…) and I have never released either.

Since I was using Visual Studio 2013 for the codding of the Source (I had no idea what I was doing at the time) dad notice it and showed me C# and making Window form apps. I still remember that one night when I finished making my calculator, still have it as well!

So, at the point I was sort of into Programming. Even though no-one except my dad could understand anything. But in 2016, my first year of high school I meet a friend called Panc4kes and he too also sort of knew codding(maybe not too much at that point? Idk) and we would play Minecraft on the school computers every lunch, but the school also had something else on the school computers, it was called Unity. He told me that this program could make games, so what did you know? We launched it up and tried to make a cube move… we failed.

But after that I went home and installed Unity Personal on my PC and created something. I don’t remember what I created, and I don’t even have any of the source code for any of my old projects, basically anything below 2017 (except Revenge and that calculator!) , since that they are all sitting on an old raid 5 set of hard drives from my oldish pc. I still have them, might try to get all the stuff from them later in life. But anyway in 2018 I created probably one of my best game called ‘Team Capture’ using Unity. This game, like Revenge was never release nor is it finished. But I am proud of what is in it!

In mid-2018 I stumbled across a video called ‘Making a Discord BOT - #1’. He showed on how to make a Discord bot and it was in C#. So of course I created a Discord bot and at first it was called ‘DiscordEpicBot’, I know right, so a unique and special name. Eventually it turned into ‘Pootis-Bot’ and I decided that I should release the source code on Github, and that’s what I did.

Wow that’s it, well obviously there is more than, but that is all I want to say about it!
